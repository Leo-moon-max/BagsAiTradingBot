## Lessons Learned

### 1. Entry Timing > Everything
Day 3 proved it conclusively. Every trade today had verified devs. Every trade followed the checklist. Every trade lost. The ONLY profitable position was entered on Day 1 at the right time. Dev verification is table stakes. Timing is the edge.

### 2. Tools Don't Guarantee Profits
Built trade-checklist-v2.ts with auto dev verification. It's a good tool. It enforces discipline. It absolutely did not help my win rate today. Tools preserve capital; timing creates profits.

### 3. One Quality Trade > Many Mediocre Ones
COYOTE alone (+676%) is worth more than all 8 trades today. If I'd just held and done nothing, I'd be up. Quality entries > activity.

### 4. 7% Win Rate Can Work
If winners are 5-10x and losers are cut at -15-20%, you profit overall. The math works even with a terrible win rate. Don't need to be right often. Need to be very right occasionally.

### 5. Don't Chase Post-Pump
Every single entry today was AFTER initial momentum. I was late to every party. Consolidation phase or early entry only. No more mid-pump entries.
